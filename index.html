Manta Ray

Nicknamed “Devil Rays” for their “horns” which are actually extensions of the fins that assist in funneling plankton into their mouths.

  • Scientific Name: Manta Birostris
  • Average Length: 2,980 lbs
  • Average Lifespan: Approximately 20 years
  • Habitat: Tropical, subtropical and warm temperate oceans

Manta rays have quite the striking appearance with its black and white coloration and enormous size with a wingspan of 20+ feet. Manta rays are split into two distinct species, manta birostris and manta alfredi. They're distributed between tropical and subtropical waters of the Indian and Pacific oceans with rare sightings in the Atlantic ocean.
